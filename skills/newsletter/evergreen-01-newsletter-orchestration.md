# Evergreen Skill 01: Newsletter Orchestration

Goal: Orchestrate the full newsletter workflow end-to-end.

Sequence:
1. Confirm objective, audience, and desired outcome.
2. Extract key inputs from uploaded files.
3. Propose an outline and editorial angle.
4. Draft the newsletter with clear sections and flow.
5. Provide subject lines, preview text, and CTAs.
6. Run quality checks and revise if needed.

Output expectations:
- Clear headline and opening hook.
- Structured sections with scannable formatting.
- Practical takeaways and next steps.
